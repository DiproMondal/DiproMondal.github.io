Hobbies & Interests

Gaming

I am an avid PC gamer and enjoy exploring different genres, mainly story-driven RPGs and FPS games. Gaming is not only entertainment for me, but also a way to challenge myself, connect with others, and experience immersive narratives.

Anime

I enjoy watching anime, especially series with strong storytelling, bountiful action, and creative world-building. Anime has been both a source of relaxation and inspiration, often offering unique perspectives and ideas that influence my creative thinking.

Painting & 3D Modelling

I used to paint extensively, which later grew into an interest in 3D modelling. While I no longer have the time to explore these activities as much, they nurtured my creativity and continue to influence the way I approach both work and hobbies.
