About Me

I am Dipro Mondal, a PhD candidate in Statistics at Maastricht University at the Department of Methodology and Statistics, expected to graduate in December 2025. My research is about planning reliability studies with quantitative outcomes, which focuses on the development of sample size methods, particularly using the intraclass correlation coefficient (ICC).

My work emphasizes methodological planning, uncertainty quantification, and reproducible research practices. Alongside methodological contributions, I provide R Shiny apps to make the methods accessible to researchers.

I hold two MSc degrees, one in Data Science from Leiden University and another in Physics from IISER Kolkata.
